Hot German Potato Salad

Description

This a great salad recipe that will leave you wanting more.

It's really easy to make and can easily be made in large batches for those potlucks.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 apples — peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • ½ cup raisins (Optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, and salt together in a serving bowl.
  3. Stir in apples, celery, walnuts, and raisins. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
